I would recommend **keeping this Action Log from now on**. For a Horizon Europe proposal it becomes extremely useful, as it provides a chronological record of consortium decisions, technical evolution, partner confirmations and action items. By proposal submission, you'll likely have **40–60 such entries**, making it easy to trace why architectural and consortium decisions were made.
