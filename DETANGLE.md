### Action 19 👉 What is the difference between:

- T2.1 → collects stakeholder problems, needs, use cases

#### Internal clarification – T2.1 vs T2.3
- T2.1 (Use Cases): Captures stakeholder problems, needs, and operational scenarios in a solution-agnostic manner.
- Purpose: Understand context, actors, and expectations before defining system obligations.
- T2.3 (Requirements): Converts validated needs and use cases into structured, traceable, and testable requirement statements.
- Purpose: Support prioritisation, technical alignment, and mapping to DETANGLE outputs and deliverables.
- Link: Use cases and stakeholder inputs from T2.1 serve as sources for T2.3 requirement formulation under the phased methodology.
